ABOUT THE CAFE

The Cafe was owned and first started in Metro Manila by Ms.Rachelle Kyle D. due to her unending love for coffee , and also because her family loves to drink coffee to the mere fact that even on a sunny day her family or friends would always find comfort in a cup, at first it was just for fun and soon became known by many so she decided to expand the business across the metro.

In addition to our full espresso and brew bar menu, we serve fresh made-to-order breakfast and lunch sandwiches, as well as a selection of sides and salads and other good stuff.

"Use products from nature for what it's worth - but never too early, nor too late." Fresh is the new sweet.

Freelance Web Designer, Architect, Coffeeist and Owner: Rachelle Kyle D.

Opening hours: everyday from 6am to 5pm.

Address: MARIKINA CITY, PHILIPPINES
